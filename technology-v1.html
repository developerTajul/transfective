Technology

By Methodically Facilitating Companies, Transfective Enables Them To Gain International Experience. We Aim To Empower Global Brands In Various Ways Through Language, Community And Ethnicity.

Our Solution

Translation API

If you have content stored on your server or within a content management system (CMS) that requires dynamic translation, our Translation API (application programming interface) is your go to. Our Translation API enables you to send through documents and source strings from your system on our homepage in a secure manner, allowing you to avoid the hassle of manual project submission.

Translation Memory Cloud

Our Translation memory builds a database based on previously translated sentences and common phrases, allowing for reduced cost and an overall sped up translation process. Through the Translation Memory Cloud, collective benefits scale at a rapid pace and apply to all projects.

Glossary Management

Through our strict glossary management protocol, we ensure the translation produced is consistent and utilizes the adequate terminology. The glossary is created through a collaboration with your teams, enabling us to understand and store the preferred key terminology in the source language, as well as its approved translation in the target language. To aid the linguists, certain notes and descriptions detailing the context are also incorporated.

CAT Tools

Transfective can utilize previously translated content stored within the translation memory through utilizing computer assisted translation (CAT Tools). This process provides organizations with more options for translation, allowing them to save cost, secure improved timelines and have their stylistic requirements addressed. Since glossaries can be built, updated and maintained overtime, the benefits are ever evolving.

Machine Translation

Incorporating Artificial Intelligence into global content management is a step that has revolutionized the localization process. The language service industry is a major proponent in the advancement of neural machine translation technology. Transfective aims to assist clients embrace advancements and future-proof their ever-evolving content universe by utilizing the most optimal machine translation technology, coupled with the best practice approaches for optimizing content and the business processes.

Manage High Volumes

Machine Translation allows you to manage large volumes of content in a relatively short span of time. A practical and affordable solution is provided for times when translation is essential, but the budget or timeline is strict. With a range of different MT technologies as well as light or heavy post editing options, we ensure that your needs for quality, speed and cost are addressed.

Content Review Solutions

Through Transfective’s dynamic solutions, the obstacles that come with coordinating a thorough content review can be overcome. We offer a cloud-based platform that facilitates translation reviews in a simplistic manner from anywhere in the world, at any time. Changes, new submissions and deliveries are monitored very strictly and audit records as well as user-level permissions eradicate the manual process completely. The review timelines are also expedited.

Shorten Time to Market

A stable MT strategy can prove to provide endless advantages in a highly competitive setting. A MT engine with adequate human post editing can produce structured content at a rapid pace that encompasses various quality ideals, comparable to a fully human process. Especially when it comes to publishing online content, where speed is of the essence, clients can choose to publish draft MT content, then follow-up with post editing to further enhance the quality.

Optimize
Translation Investment

For content that is high in volume or ever changing, Transfective provides MT and post-edited MT – this is a more affordable option that enables clients to target wider audiences. Furthermore, MT can also provide a cost-effective approach to expanding language capabilities in business areas that are difficult to scale, such as customer support.

CMS/E-Commerce/Database Integration

Transfective allows organizations to control their multilingual content fully, while still working with their existing back-end system. By acting as a bridge between these systems, our industry leading localization technology simplifies the process of managing multilingual content. With Transfective, you can extend the benefits that come with choosing the most appropriate CMS, DMS, PIM, DAM, E-Commerce or marketing automation system since we have an ever-evolving integration system, with compatibility with all major back-end platforms.
